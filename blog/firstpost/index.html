Class Project Idea. Unnamed History Android App.

  • 01 April 2024
  • class project

The general idea is to create a gamified history app for android, that connects to a database of real historical facts. The target audience is children, aiming to engage them with history in a unique way.

The reason I have chosen to develop an Android application is because of my interest in the components of Android development and a desire to understand the Kotlin programming language. I believe through starting with a small project, I will be able to understand the fundamentals of connecting databases to a working application and getting hands-on experience with development tools.
